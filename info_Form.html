STEP 1: Check if Your Plugin is Active

  1. Open your web browser
  2. Go to: http://localhost/wordpress/wp-admin
  3. Login with your WordPress username and password
  4. Look in the left sidebar for "Echo5 Leads"
‚úÖ If you see "Echo5 Leads" in the sidebar: Plugin is active! Continue to Step 2.
‚ùå If you DON'T see "Echo5 Leads":
1. Go to Plugins ‚Üí Installed Plugins
2. Find "Echo5 Leads Manager"
3. Click "Activate"
4. Refresh the page

STEP 2: Check Plugin Settings

  1. Click on "Echo5 Leads" in the left sidebar
  2. Click on "Settings"
  3. Leave "Webhook Secret" empty for now (easier for testing)
  4. Set "SLA Hours" to 4
  5. Add your email in "Manager Email(s)"
  6. Click "Save Settings"
You should see a settings page with fields for Webhook Secret, SLA Hours, etc.

STEP 3: Create a Simple Contact Form

We'll use Contact Form 7 since you mentioned you have it:

  1. Go to Contact ‚Üí Contact Forms
  2. Click "Add New"
  3. Give it a title like "Lead Test Form"
  4. Replace the form code with this:
<label> Your Name (required) [text* your-name] </label> <label> Your Email (required) [email* your-email] </label> <label> Your Phone [tel your-phone] </label> <label> Your City [text your-city] </label> <label> Your Message [textarea your-message] </label> [submit "Send"]
  1. Click "Save"
  2. Copy the shortcode (something like [contact-form-7 id="123"])

STEP 4: Add Form to a Page

  1. Go to Pages ‚Üí Add New
  2. Title: "Test Lead Form"
  3. In the content area, paste your Contact Form 7 shortcode
  4. Click "Publish"
  5. Click "View Page" to see your form
You should now see a contact form on your page with name, email, phone, city, and message fields.

STEP 5: Test the Form (The Fun Part!)

  1. Fill out your form with test data:
    • Name: John Doe
    • Email: john.doe@test.com
    • Phone: 555-123-4567
    • City: Test City
    • Message: This is a test message
  2. Click "Send"
  3. You should see a success message
üí° Tip: Try submitting 2-3 different test leads with different names and emails.

STEP 6: Check if Leads Were Captured

  1. Go back to your WordPress admin: http://localhost/wordpress/wp-admin
  2. Click "Echo5 Leads" in the sidebar
  3. Click "Leads"
  4. You should see your test leads in a table!
‚úÖ If you see your leads: Congratulations! Your plugin is working perfectly!
‚ùå If you don't see any leads: See troubleshooting section below.

STEP 7: View Lead Details

  1. In the leads list, click "View Details" on one of your leads
  2. You should see all the lead information
  3. Look for the "Activities" section
  4. You should see notes like "Attribution v1" and possibly "UTM snapshot"
This shows that the new features are working - attribution tracking and activity logging!

üîß TROUBLESHOOTING

Problem: No leads appear after form submission

Solutions to try:
1. Check if Contact Form 7 plugin is active
2. Go to Settings ‚Üí Permalinks ‚Üí Click "Save Changes"
3. Check WordPress error logs (ask your hosting provider)
4. Try deactivating and reactivating the Echo5 Leads plugin

Problem: Form doesn't submit

Solutions to try:
1. Check that Contact Form 7 is properly installed
2. Make sure the form shortcode is correct
3. Check browser console for JavaScript errors (F12 key)

Problem: Can't see Echo5 Leads in admin

Solutions to try:
1. Go to Plugins ‚Üí Installed Plugins
2. Find "Echo5 Leads Manager" and click "Activate"
3. If it's not there, re-upload the plugin folder
